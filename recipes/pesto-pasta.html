Pesto Pasta Recipe

This chicken pesto pasta is a date night-worthy dinner recipe that’s ready in 20 minutes or less. Make this luxurious pasta when you’re craving a warm bowl of creamy, Italian-style comfort.

Ingredients

Below is a list and quantaties of ingredients required to make this recipe

  • Chicken - 1 breast per portion
  • Penne pasta 70gram per portion
  • Pesto - 3 Tbsp
  • Heavy cream - 100ml
  • parmesan - handful
  • Salt and Pepper - To taste
    • nano
  • Olive oil - 1 Tbsp

Step by Step Guide to creating this dish

  1. Bring water to the boil and add the pasta
  2. Season and cook the chicken in Olive oil tossing around the pan for around 7 to 8 minutes
  3. When the pasta is cooked drain but keep 1 cup of pasta water to help make the auce
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the cooked chicken in
  5. Add the cream and parmesan cheese in small amounts to create the sauce, if the sauce is looking abit dry add in a splash of pasta water until the pasta is glossy and coated in creamy sauce
  6. Take the pan off the heat and add the pesto, keep stirring while adding this ensuring everything is mixed well
